Cyrus UI is a new kind of Roblox interface framework designed for developers who want their games to look cutting-edge and full of futuristic, cyberpunk flair. It’s built in Lua, so it slides right into your workflow. Think glowing neon buttons, smooth animations, and all the interactive pieces you need—toggles, sliders, dropdowns, textboxes, notifications—working together as a sleek, unified control panel that makes any game feel instantly upgraded.

Every bit of Cyrus UI is tuned for that high-tech vibe. You get deep, dark backgrounds that make bright neon accents pop—colors like cyan, magenta, and electric blue. The whole thing feels alive and energetic, straight out of a cyberpunk city. Tabs slide in with that satisfying glide, sliders and toggles bounce back when you move them, and notifications show up with a flash. Everything just looks and feels dynamic.

But Cyrus UI isn’t just about looking cool—it pulls players right into the world you’re building. Whether you’re building a game hub, admin tools, a roleplay interface, or a slick futuristic menu, this framework turns basic Roblox UIs into interactive, neon-lit command centers. Every click, every toggle, every swipe feels like you’re using a piece of futuristic tech instead of just navigating menus.

Developers get a modular system that’s actually fun to use—add tabs, sections, and interactive parts in seconds. The look always stays consistent and stylish. Even small UI touches like notifications or dropdowns make a big difference, wrapping players deeper into your game’s high-tech atmosphere.

Cyrus UI isn’t just another GUI kit. It’s a toolbox for building bold, cyberpunk-inspired experiences on Roblox—a perfect pick for creators who want their games to stand out with style, speed, and serious interactivity.
